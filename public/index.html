WOW

(Widows and/or Widowers)

We are WOW (Widows and/or Widowers). We are a social support group for people who have lost a spouse.

We meet 2 times a month on the 1st and 3rd Wednesday at noon in the Community Room at Hy-Vee, 2700 DeKalb Avenue, Sycamore, IL 60178.

You are welcome! We are not a dating group.

For more info, call Flo: 815-751-7532 (wow@reagan.com)
